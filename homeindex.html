About Us

The website functions as a comprehensive medical record management platform, ensuring seamless access to your medical history in emergency situations, thereby obviating the need for hospitals to manually retrieve pertinent documents.

Identification

Ways that will be used to identify the use..

Face Recognition

A facial recognition system is an advanced technology designed to compare a human face captured in a digital image or video frame with a database of faces, enabling precise identification and authentication.

Fingerprint Recognition

Fingerprint recognition systems operate by analyzing the unique patterns and ridges present on a finger when it is pressed against a smooth surface.

Retina Recognition

Retina recognition is a biometric technique that employs the distinctive patterns found in a person's retina to identify and authenticate individuals.

Portals

Our website will feature two distinct portals: one accessible to the general public, providing essential information and resources; the other exclusively accessible to hospitals, offering restricted access tailored for medical professionals to manage and review patient data securely.

  • User Gateway
  • Hospital Gateway

User Gateway

In this section, individuals will be able to input their vital and critical medical history along with emergency contact numbers to ensure accurate and comprehensive data management.


Moreover, individuals will have the option to provide their biometric data, such as fingerprint, facial scan, or retina scan, facilitated by the website's integrated scanners. This additional layer of security ensures seamless and accurate data input for enhanced identification and authentication purposes.

Hospital Gateway

In this section, in the event of accidents or emergencies, the patient's identity can be swiftly confirmed through fingerprint, face, or retina scan. This enables immediate access to the patient's comprehensive medical history stored on the website.

Additionally, the hospital can promptly contact the patient's family members using the provided emergency contact information, ensuring timely communication and necessary support during critical situations.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Nature or purpose of the website?

    Our website serves as a comprehensive medical history app manager, ensuring that your vital health records are readily accessible in emergencies. By centralizing your medical information on our platform, hospitals can seamlessly retrieve your medical history without the need for manual document searches, facilitating efficient and informed medical care when time is of the essence.

  • How can one ensure that his/her data is safe?

    Additionally, our dual-user section guarantees the utmost security of your data, ensuring that only authorized personnel from the hospital can access your information and medical history. This stringent security measure safeguards your privacy and confidentiality, providing peace of mind knowing that your sensitive healthcare data is protected against unauthorized access.

  • How to feed medical history?

    You can feed your medical history from the admin page in the medical details section whenever you want. Also whenever you would be admitted to hospital if there is any problem that the hospital considers that it should be contained in your medical history then will feed that data on their own in the app.
